## Sample Responses to Common Questions

**Q: "How often will you update the data?"**
A: "We're committing to quarterly updates for contributed projects, with ad-hoc updates for major changes (new audits, team changes, etc.)"

**Q: "What about projects that shut down?"**
A: "Our validation pipeline flags deprecated projects. We'll update status and add sunset date transparently."

**Q: "Will you maintain this long-term?"**
A: "Yes - we're committed to maintaining the 100+ projects we contribute. We see this as ongoing community contribution, not one-time data dump."

**Q: "What if we disagree on categorization?"**
A: "Your taxonomy takes precedence! We map to your categories. Where there's ambiguity, we're happy to discuss or defer to your judgment."

**Q: "How do you handle privacy for team members?"**
A: "We only include publicly available information from official sources (LinkedIn, GitHub, company websites). If someone wants removal, we'll immediately comply."

**Q: "What license is your data under?"**
A: "We're flexible! We can match your license (likely ODbL) to ensure compatibility."
