# GnuPG - Team Report

## Organization

### g10 Code GmbH
- **Type**: German company (GmbH)
- **Founded**: 2001
- **Managing Director**: Werner Koch
- **Purpose**: Primary GnuPG development employer

### GnuPG e.V.
- **Type**: German nonprofit (eingetragener Verein)
- **Purpose**: Charitable organization for collecting donations
- **Status**: Recognized for tax-deductible donations

## Leadership

### Werner Koch (Founder)
- **Born**: July 11, 1961
- **Role**: Principal author, g10 Code managing director
- **Background**: 29 years commercial software experience
- **Started GnuPG**: 1997 after attending Richard Stallman talk
- **Location**: Erkrath, near Dusseldorf, Germany
- **Awards**: Award for Advancement of Free Software 2015
- **Other**: Co-founder of FSFE

## Funding History

### 2015 Crisis
- Werner Koch earned ~$25,000/year average over 14 years
- ProPublica article (Feb 5, 2015) exposed funding struggles
- Result: 120,000 EUR raised from individuals
- Linux Foundation: $60,000 one-time grant
- Facebook: $50,000/year commitment
- Stripe: $50,000/year commitment

### Current Model
- **Recurring donations** prioritized over one-time
- **Corporate sponsors**: Linux Foundation, Stripe, Facebook
- **Commercial**: GnuPG VS-Desktop (BSI-certified product)
- **Philosophy**: Majority from individuals, not corporations
