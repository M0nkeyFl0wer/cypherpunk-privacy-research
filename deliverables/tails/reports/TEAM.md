# Tails OS - Team Report

## Organization
- **Current**: Part of The Tor Project (merged September 2024)
- **Previous**: Independent project with voluntary board
- **Team Size**: Small group across multiple continents

## Leadership

| Name | Role |
|------|------|
| intrigeri | Tails Team Lead |
| Isabela Fernandes | Executive Director, Tor Project (parent org) |

## History
- **2009**: Project initiated by pseudonymous developers
- **Original name**: "amnesia" - merged with Incognito project
- **September 2024**: Merged into Tor Project
- Donation infrastructures merging in 2025

## Merger Rationale
- Tails had outgrown its existing structure
- Merging relieved stress of handling fundraising, finances, HR independently
- intrigeri: "In a way, it feels like coming home"

## Notable Users/Endorsements

- **DuckDuckGo**: Ongoing supporter
